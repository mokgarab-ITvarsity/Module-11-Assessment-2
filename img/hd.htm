About Fortuna Foods

Fortuna Foods offers readily available premium products that are even fresher than the beats dropped in your local club. We never drop our quality standards and provide a continuous supply of mouth-watering products. We keep our promise of freshness by supporting a network of local and global farms that ensure we bring the best to you, every time.

Our facilities are designed specifically to grade, process and pack our products efficiently while adhering to the most stringent food safety protocols.  At Fortuna Foods, we are the whole package, with the added value of being able to store bulk product in our on-site cooling rooms, specifically designed to keep us (and by default ‘you’) the coolest foodies in town.

Join the list of food retailers, manufacturers and food-service distributors that are currently enjoying our bulk and Consumer Packaged Goods.  Location is not an issue; we are centrally located for distribution convenience and can even export your very own private-label CPGs.

The question is, how can we help?

We can supply products whole, peeled, sliced, diced, pickled, caramelised or roasted.

Interested in something else?

Allow us to sweep you off your feet with our delicious pestos, pastes and sauces.
